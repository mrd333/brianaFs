Who Is Sleeping With Sirens?

Sleeping With Sirens is an American band from Orlando, Florida. There are five members in the band:

  • Kellin Quinn: Vocals
  • Nick Martin: Guitar
  • Justin Hills: Bass
  • Jack Fowler: Lead Guitar
  • Gabe Barham: Drums

When Did They Become Sleeping With Sirens?

Sleeping With Sirens formed in 2009 with starting memebers Kellin Quinn, Alex Kaladjian,Brandon McMasters, Justin Hills, and Gabe Barham.
